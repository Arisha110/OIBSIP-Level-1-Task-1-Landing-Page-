About Us

With the endeavour to provide a dynamic learning environment to children and to inculcate a spirit of enquiry and creativity, cooperation and mutual respect among them, Sagar Public School set up its first branch at Saket Nagar, Bhopal in the year 2001. In the last 22 years, SPS has shown exponential growth in all respects. With six branches, and an enrolment of over 11000+ students, SPS stands distinct as one of the largest schools in central India.
